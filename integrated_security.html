Automation services

  • Integrated Security System

    Integrating your security system with your home/building automation system provides the extra security of utilizing your lighting and window shades as additional deterrents. Integrated Security Systems are easily programmed to activate motion sensors, security cameras & alarm system whether you are at home/building or away.

    Whenever required, you can automatically lock all the doors, activate motion sensors and security cameras, and turn on exterior night lights with the simple press on your smart phones or pads.

    Benefits of Integrating your Security System with your Home Automation System:

    • It works around the clock to keep you safe.
    • Your Valuables Will Be Protected
    • Home Security Systems Are A Valuable Crime Deterrent
    • You Will Be Immediately Notified Of Any Fire Or Gas Problems
    • View your security cameras and systems worldwide through your smart phone and tablet device.
