  • Internet of Things

    The IoT allows objects to be sensed and/or controlled remotely across existing network infrastructure, creating opportunities for more direct integration of the physical world into computer-based systems, and resulting in improved efficiency, accuracy and economic benefit.
    The growth of IoT has found that its ecosystem including its components like devices, analytics, systems, network and security will connect to and control IoT devices in different environments including health, smart connected homes, transportation and even agriculture.
    Newtok helps our clients to realize the potential of IoT, finding the right end-to-end solutions that integrate hardware, software, sensors and cloud services to maximize the easiness to your operations.
    Newtok see the IoT as an incredible opportunity to bridge the physical and digital worlds for business transformation. Our IT and Automation engineers work together to make and connect physical products, processes, and places to bridge the physical and digital worlds, bringing the vision of the “Internet of Things” to life.
    Our team is a highly motivated group of peoples focused on inventing and contributing to the ecosystems of new and emerging technologies that bring the Internet of Things to real life.
