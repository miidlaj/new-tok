Edunok

Simplify your school routine management

Our Edunok School software automates all of the mundane tasks of running a school into a powerful easy-to-use software system. This way you can spend less time managing your dojobs and more time training your students!

Our Edunok School software supports various platform including Cloud Computing, Mobility (Android, iPhone), Web technologies and more!

Edunok is integrate with our Smart Class room solutions like Interactive Class rooms, E-learning, Automatic attendance taking etc.

Powerful features for your school do jobs

Our Edunok School software gives you all of the tools you need to run your Smart Classes incorporating state of art automation tools and technologies and manage your school smoothly and efficiently. You can easily monitor your student’s indicators, track your student’s location, set up assignments, tests & events and monitor the performances indexes of your students.

Edunok School software has separate modules for Principal, School Administration, Teachers and Student/Guardian.

Cloud based installation

Install Edunok School Software on our cloud server and access data of any of your schools from any location. All you need is a steady Internet connection. Run Edunok from many locations synchronized to single database.

Scalable

Edunok School software is scalable to be installed on very small net books to large array of computers.
