  • Smart Class Room Solutions

    Newtok Automation provides smart class room solutions for schools and institutions. Smart Classroom is modern, innovative and interactive teaching and learning environment that has available technological equipment to enhance the students' engagements. Our Smart Classroom solution is based on interactive, which illuminates abstract & difficult concepts with crystal clarity. It eliminates the learning gaps between teacher & students and helps them realize their true potential.

    Smart Classroom is equipped with the following equipment:

    • Classroom Projector
    • Smart Interactive WhiteBoard
    • Smart Teaching Podium
    • Smart Document Camera
    • Sound System
    • Microphone (wireless hand-held)
    • e-learning Software
    • A desktop/laptop acting as the central system
