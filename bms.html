Automation services

  • BMS (Building Management System)

    Newtok provide effective solutions for making your buildings intelligent and saving energy at the same time. Our services includes fully integrated building management systems incorporating HVAC (heating, ventilation and air conditioning); Lighting; Energy monitoring; Access control & Security.

    In an average commercial building lighting and HVAC account for around 70% of the total energy consumption. This enormous percentage represents a vast amount of capital, as well as a large detrimental environmental impact. Our automation solutions will help you save energy of at least 30%.

    Our BMS team looking to 'network and integrate' with every possible device in the building, monitor and collect every possible data as possible, and shows integrated intelligent dashboards for insight into the energy behavior of their building, at different times of the day/week/month/year, as well as in different areas. Using the same networked system can also manage loads based on more insight and intelligence.

    Newtok BMS solution will help the property owners to integrate all of the following to monitor the entire building.

    • Illumination (lighting) control
    • Electric power control
    • Heating, ventilation and air-conditioning (HVAC)
    • Security and observation
    • Access control
    • Fire alarm system
    • Lifts, elevators etc.
    • Plumbing
    • Closed-circuit television (CCTV)
    • Other engineering systems
    • Control Panel
    • PA system
    • Alarm Monitor
    • Security Automation
