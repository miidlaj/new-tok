Automation services

  • Lighting Control

    A smart lighting control system will save electricity and save your money. Newtok Lighting Automation can enhance the enjoyment and property value of any home and business. Our lighting control systems give you the power to remotely control all of your lighting from a single screen, wherever you are.

    Our lighting controls can also integrate with other building systems like Security, Fire, HVAC etc. This integration provides additional security and operating efficiencies that optimize energy consumption.

    Different Lighting solutions we offers

    • Wireless Lighting
    • Voice-Controlled Lighting
    • Centralized Lighting
    • Exterior Lighting

    Benefits of Lighting Control

    • Smart Lighting adds elegance, ambiance, convenience and energy efficiency to your property.
    • A room can have multiple scenes pre-set, each one created for different activities in the room.
    • Longer lamp life is also gained when dimming and switching off lights when not in use.
    • Wireless lighting control systems provide additional benefits including reduced installation costs and increased flexibility over where switches and sensors may be placed.
