  • VoIP Development

    Newtok offers IP telephony and Voice over IP solutions (IPBX, IVR, Softphones, CRM integration etc) based on open standards and Open Source platforms such as Asterisk, FreeSwitch, OpenSIPs etc.
    We offers services for small offices to large multi-site companies. Our focus is to deliver low costs solutions by using as much as possible Open-Source software and technologies.

    Maximum Functionality with Minimum Cost

    Our IP-PBX is a complete Unified Communication system with advanced features such as unified messaging (Voice-mail, Fax, Email), IVR, Call recording, video conferencing, integration with IMs such as Skype, Gtalk, support for multiple companies on one platform. Our solutions are ideal for small, medium and large organizations and reduces the investment cost, space requirements and effort to manage the system.

    Interoperable by integrating with existing business applications

    The system can be integrated with your existing software systems like CRM, ERP, websites, other IT platforms to automate services like reservation system, wakeup call, metered billing etc.
